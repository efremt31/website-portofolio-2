ERITREA

COME EXPLORE AFRICA'S HIDDEN JEWEL

Travel to Eritrea provides one the opportunity to see and learn more about one of the most isolated countries on the planet, where a society is dominated and run by a very strict and controlling government. Not that many people are brave enough to explore this mysterious nation, but if you do intend to visit, know that you are in for a real, authentic African experience; the country's charms lie in its natural attractions, wonderful architecture, dramatic scenery, and highly friendly and hospitable people.
